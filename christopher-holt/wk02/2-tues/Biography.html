CV and History

  • Born in Edmonton, AB, Canada on 21st July, 1911
  • He went to the University of Manitoba and Cambridge
  • Taught at the University's Wisconsin, St Louis, Assumption and St Michael's College
  • Chairman of Ford Foundation Seminar on Culture and Communication
  • Co-Editor of Explorations magazine
  • Director of Project in Understanding New Media for National Association of Educational Broadcasters and U.S. Office of Education
  • Created a new Centre for Culture and Technology to study the psychic and social consequences of technologies and media
  • Died in his sleep 31st December, 1980
