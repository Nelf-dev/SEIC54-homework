John Von Neumann

von Neumanns Hometown

John von Neumann was born in Budapest in 1903.

Budapest at the time was undergoign significant transformation in the fields of Science and Engineering. It hosted several of the world's most prestigious universities, including Eötvös Loránd University.

von Neumann himself attended Eötvös Loránd, University of Berlin and UTH Zurich.

Budapest

  • One of the largest and most powerful empires in the world.
