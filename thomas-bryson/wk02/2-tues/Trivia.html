John Von Neumann

Trivia




von Neumann was an avid party-goer and liked to host wild dinner parties throughout his academic life.



von Neumann spoke 8 languages. Hungarian, French, German and English, Italian, Yiddish, Ancient Latin and Greek



von Neumann was apparently a terrible driver.

Life Achievements

  • Largely considered worthy of roughly 3 Nobel prizes in his areas of discovery if he had lived long enough

  • Created the theory of Cellular Automata, or the quantised and programmable study of life from an initial state.

  • One of the chief mathematicians and physicists on the Manhatten Project
